About A Team Guard

Established 13 years ago, A Team Guard embarked on a mission within the public sector. Four pivotal years in, we identified a niche in the private security landscape and ventured forth. This step came with round-the-clock demands, yet we rose to the occasion.

From an initial team of 50, we've expanded to a robust force of 350 specialists. While our roots began just outside Bangkok, we're now entrusted with safeguarding prestigious developments in the heart of the Sukhumvit area. Our transformation from a fledgling firm to Bangkok's premier security authority underscores our dedication and vision for a more secure community.
